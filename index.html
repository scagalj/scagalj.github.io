About Us

JustAround is a family run restaurant, established in 1967. We are passionate about Dalmatian and Mediterranean cuisine, thus our menu is based on local products and groceries. We offer steaks and traditional seafood prepared on the charcoal grill, as well pastas, salads and desserts. Food is paired with wide selection of Croatian wines and you can also try handcrafted cocktails from our bar. Newly renovated interior of the old stone house can accommodate 40 guests and 80 more can enjoy a meal on the terrace. JustAround is perfectly located nearby by the sea, with a beautiful view and far away from the crowd.
